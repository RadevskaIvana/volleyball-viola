About Us

BEST PLACE TO PLAY

Volleyball club Viola Volley was founded in 2014 and works actively to this day. The club works with all age categories of children from 7 years and up. Every year, Viola Volley participates in state leagues where it has achieved significant results. The club has excellent cooperation with other clubs from Macedonia and has organized successful international events.

Do you want to be a professional volleyball player?

We offer training in a variety of difficulty levels that volleyball players of all skill levels can enjoy. You will learn volleyball from professionals with our competent and experienced staff.
